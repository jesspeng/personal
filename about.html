About Me

I am currently an Associate Technical Consultant for Adobe and a recent graduate of the University of Pennsylvania where I received a B.S.E. degree in Digital Media Design, a program within the School of Engineering that intersects computer science and art.

Although I studied computer graphics in college, my interests are multi-dimensional (pun intended). I have always loved art and find a way to integrate it into all of my projects. I also happen to love philosophy, U.S. Presidents trivia, listening to great music on Spotify, dancing to it if it's really good, and long runs.
