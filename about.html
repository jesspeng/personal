About Me

I am a senior at the University of Pennsylvania pursuing a B.S.E. degree in Digital Media Design, a program within the School of Engineering that intersects technology and art. This past summer, I was a VR/AR Intern at The New York Times where I developed immersive projects and 3d visualizations on the Graphics Desk. The summer before that I was a PM Intern at Molex, Inc., an electronics company based in Lisle, IL, where I created 3d content for their marketing initiatives.

Although I am primarily interested in 3d computer graphics, I have always loved art and find a way to integrate it in all of my projects. My interests, however, are multi-dimensional (pun intended). I love philosophy, U.S. Presidents trivia, listening to great music on Spotify, dancing to it if it's really good, and baking.
